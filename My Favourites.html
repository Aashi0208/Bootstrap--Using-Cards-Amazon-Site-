My Favourite Sport


Badminton is a racquet sport played using racquets to hit a shuttlecock across a net. Although it may be played with larger teams, the most common forms of the game are singles
Badminton rules
  1. Court
    2. The court is rectangular and divided into halves by a net. Courts are usually marked for both singles and doubles play
  2. Serving
    3. The server serves, the shuttlecock must pass over the short service line on the opponents' court or it will count as a fault.
  3. Scoring
    4. Each game is played to 21 points, with players scoring a point whenever they win a rally regardless of whether they served
  4. Lets
    5. If a let is called, the rally is stopped and replayed with no change to the score.
